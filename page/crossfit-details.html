CrossFit

Master Functional Fitness at High Intensity

Class Overview

Our CrossFit program focus on functional movements performed at high intensity. We combine elements of weightlifting, gymnastics, and metabolic conditioning to help you achieve the best fitness of your life.

What You'll Learn

  • Weightlifting (Snatch, Clean & Jerk, Squats)
  • Gymnastic movements (Pull-ups, Handstands, Muscle-ups)
  • Cardiovascular conditioning (Running, Rowing, Jump Rope)
  • High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

Benefits

Full Body

Target every muscle group in every workout.

Endurance

Improve metabolic capacity and stamina.
