Cracking the 'Fran' Code

Strategies to master the ultimate CrossFit benchmark

By Alex Thorne | 5 days ago

'Fran' is one of the most legendary and punishing benchmark workouts in CrossFit. Comprising 21-15-9 reps of 95lb thrusters and pull-ups, it's a test of pure intensity.

The Strategy: Pacing is Key

The mistake many athletes make is going 100% on the first set of 21. Instead, aim for a pace that allows you to complete the set of 15 with minimal rest. The set of 9 is where you empty the tank.

Efficiency in Movement

Use your legs in the thruster. Don't let your arms do all the work. On the pull-ups, focus on a tight kip or butterfly technique to save energy and time.

Mental Fortitude

When the burn kicks in during the round of 15, remind yourself that the end is near. Focus on one rep at a time and don't let the bag/barbell stay on the ground for too long.

Equipment Check:

Ensure your grip is solid and your shoes provide enough stability for the thrusters. A little bit of chalk goes a long way.
