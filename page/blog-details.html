5 Essential Lifts for Mass

Master these movements to build serious strength

By Alex Spartan | 2 days ago

Building muscle doesn't require a thousand different exercises. It requires mastering the basics. These 5 compound lifts should form the foundation of your training program.

1. The Deadlift

The king of all exercises. It targets the entire posterior chain and builds raw power.

2. The Squat

Essential for leg development and core stability. Don't skip leg day.

3. Bench Press

The classic upper body builder for chest, shoulders, and triceps.

4. Overhead Press

Builds broad shoulders and functional strength.

5. Pull-Ups

The best bodyweight exercise for back width and strength.

Pro Tip:

Focus on form before weight. Earning the right to lift heavy prevents injury and ensures longevity.
