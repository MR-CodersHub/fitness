Cardio Blast

Elevate Your Heart Rate & Melt Fat

Class Overview

Cardio Blast is a high-energy, fast-paced class designed to improve your cardiovascular endurance and burn maximum calories. Using a variety of equipment and bodyweight exercises, we keep your heart rate up from start to finish.

What You'll Experience

  • Intensive HIIT circuits
  • Plyometric and explosive movements
  • Sprints, rowing, and cycling intervals
  • Dynamic bodyweight conditioning

Benefits

Calorie Burn

Torch up to 900 calories per session.

Heart Health

Strengthen your cardiovascular system.
